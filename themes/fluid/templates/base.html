Fluency Grey

As a multi instrumentalist, Fluency has experience playing an assortment of string instruments including guitars, basses, harps, most percussion instruments, piano, synthesizer and other western key based instruments such as organs, xylophones, vibratones etc. He has experience arranging for string quartets, horn sections and writing for large ensembles. Always looking to serve the song, his primary focus in session work is always how to best support existing melodies and to add required energy and emotion.
